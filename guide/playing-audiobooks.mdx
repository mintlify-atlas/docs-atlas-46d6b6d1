Aradia's full-featured audio player provides everything you need for an immersive audiobook listening experience, from basic playback to advanced features like sleep timers and playback speed control.

## Starting Playback

There are multiple ways to start listening to an audiobook:

<Steps>
  <Step title="From audiobook details">
    Open any audiobook's detail page and tap the large **Play** button (center icon in the orange control bar). The player will open and begin playback.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resume from history">
    If you've previously started an audiobook, the app automatically remembers your position. Tapping play will resume from where you left off.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start from specific chapter">
    On the audiobook detail page, scroll to the "Audio Files" section and tap any chapter to start playback from that point.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The mini player appears at the bottom of the screen when audio is playing, allowing you to control playback from anywhere in the app.
</Tip>

## Player Interface

The full player screen displays:

- **Cover artwork** with shadow effects
- **Track information**: Current chapter title, audiobook title, and author
- **Progress bar** with current position and total duration
- **Playback controls**: Previous track, skip back 10s, play/pause, skip forward 10s, next track
- **Advanced controls**: Playback speed, volume, character tracking, skip silence, sleep timer
- **Quick actions**: Track list, equalizer, Chromecast

## Basic Playback Controls

<Accordion title="Play and Pause">
  Tap the large **Play/Pause button** in the center of the controls. The button shows a pause icon when playing and a play icon when paused.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Skip Between Chapters">
  - **Previous Chapter**: Tap the skip previous button (left of play/pause)
  - **Next Chapter**: Tap the skip next button (right of play/pause)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Skip 10 Seconds">
  - **Rewind 10s**: Tap the replay-10 button
  - **Forward 10s**: Tap the forward-10 button
  
  These are perfect for re-listening to a line or skipping past pauses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Seek to Specific Position">
  Drag the progress bar slider to jump to any position in the current chapter. Time labels show current and total duration.
</Accordion>

## Advanced Playback Features

### Playback Speed

Adjust the narration speed to match your preference:

1. Tap the **speedometer icon** in the advanced controls
2. A slider appears with speed options from **0.5x to 2.0x**
3. Drag the slider to your desired speed
4. The speed changes immediately while playing

<Note>
  The speedometer icon turns orange when playback speed is set to anything other than 1.0x.
</Note>

### Volume Control

1. Tap the **volume icon** in the advanced controls
2. Adjust the slider from 0% to 100%
3. Changes apply in real-time

### Sleep Timer

Set a timer to automatically pause playback after a specified duration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open timer options">
    Tap the **snooze icon** in the advanced controls to open the sleep timer menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select duration">
    Choose from preset durations:
    - 15 minutes
    - 30 minutes
    - 45 minutes
    - 60 minutes
    - 90 minutes
    - **End of Track**: Special option that pauses when the current chapter finishes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Timer is active">
    The snooze icon turns orange and displays the remaining time. The timer continues even if you close the app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cancel timer (optional)">
    Tap the snooze icon again while a timer is active, then select cancel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The "End of Track" timer is perfect for bedtime listening - it ensures you don't wake up mid-chapter.
</Tip>

### Skip Silence

Automatically skip long pauses in the audio:

1. Tap the **flash icon** in the advanced controls
2. When enabled, the icon turns orange
3. The player will skip extended silent passages

<Note>
  Skip Silence is useful for audiobooks with long pauses between chapters or sections.
</Note>

### Equalizer Settings

Customize audio frequency response:

1. Tap the **equalizer icon** in the top-right corner
2. Adjust frequency bands to enhance voice clarity or bass response
3. Settings are saved and persist across sessions

## Track List and Navigation

1. Tap the **list icon** in the top-right corner to view all chapters
2. The track list shows:
   - Chapter numbers and titles
   - Current playing position (highlighted)
   - Chapter durations
3. Tap any chapter to jump directly to it

## Character Tracking

For audiobooks with multiple characters:

1. Tap the **people icon** in the advanced controls
2. View and manage character information
3. Track character appearances throughout the story

<Note>
  Character tracking availability depends on the audiobook's metadata.
</Note>

## Chromecast Support

Stream audiobooks to compatible devices:

1. Ensure your Chromecast device is on the same network
2. Tap the **Chromecast icon** in the top-right corner
3. Select your device from the list
4. Playback transfers to your selected device

## Background Playback

Aradia continues playing when:
- The app is minimized
- The screen is locked
- You're using other apps

Use your device's notification controls or lock screen controls to manage playback without opening the app.
