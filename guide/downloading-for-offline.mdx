Download audiobooks to your device for offline listening. Perfect for commutes, flights, or areas with poor connectivity.

## How to Download an Audiobook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open audiobook details">
    Browse or search for an audiobook, then tap it to open the details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start download">
    Tap the **download icon** in the orange control bar (left icon, next to the play button).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor progress">
    The download begins immediately. You'll see:
    - A progress notification showing download percentage
    - The ability to continue browsing while downloading
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download completes">
    Once finished, the audiobook is available for offline playback. The download icon changes to indicate the audiobook is already downloaded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Downloads include all audio files for the audiobook plus metadata (cover art, description, chapter information).
</Note>

## Managing Downloads

Access your downloaded audiobooks through the Downloads page:

### Viewing Downloads

1. Tap the **Downloads** icon in the bottom navigation bar
2. Your downloads are organized into three sections:
   - **Active Downloads**: Currently downloading or paused
   - **Failed Downloads**: Downloads that encountered errors
   - **Completed**: Successfully downloaded audiobooks

### Download Status Indicators

Each download shows:
- **Audiobook title** and cover art
- **Progress bar** (for active downloads)
- **Download date** (for completed downloads)
- **Error message** (for failed downloads)
- **Status icon**:
  - Downloading arrow (blue) - actively downloading
  - Pause icon (orange) - download paused
  - Checkmark (green) - download complete
  - Alert icon (red) - download failed

### Canceling Downloads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find active download">
    Open the Downloads page and locate the audiobook in the "Active Downloads" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cancel">
    Tap the **X icon** next to the download progress.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The download stops immediately and any partial files are removed from your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Playing Downloaded Audiobooks

Once downloaded, audiobooks work offline:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open from Downloads">
    Go to the Downloads page and tap any completed audiobook.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Playback starts offline">
    The audiobook details page opens with all your downloaded content. No internet connection is required.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Full offline functionality">
    All features work offline including:
    - Chapter navigation
    - Playback speed control
    - Sleep timer
    - Equalizer settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Downloaded audiobooks also appear in your normal library and can be accessed from the home screen just like streaming content.
</Tip>

## Deleting Downloads

Free up storage space by removing downloaded audiobooks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Downloads">
    Open the Downloads page from the bottom navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find the audiobook">
    Locate the audiobook in the "Completed" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete">
    Tap the **trash icon** next to the audiobook title.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    A dialog appears asking for confirmation. Tap **Delete** to remove all downloaded files.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Deleting a download removes it from your device but keeps it in your favorites (if you've added it) and in your playback history. You can re-download it anytime.
</Note>

## Managing Download Errors

If a download fails:

<Accordion title="Check storage space">
  Ensure your device has enough free storage. Audiobooks can range from a few hundred MB to several GB depending on length and quality.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verify connection">
  Failed downloads often result from interrupted internet connections. Check your WiFi or mobile data connection.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Retry failed downloads">
  In the "Failed Downloads" section, tap the **refresh icon** to retry downloading the audiobook.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clear and restart">
  If retrying doesn't work, delete the failed download and start fresh from the audiobook's detail page.
</Accordion>

## Finding Downloaded Files

Downloaded audiobooks are stored in your device's app directory:

1. Open the Downloads page
2. Tap the **folder icon** in the top-right corner
3. A message displays the storage path (typically `/Android/data/com.aradia.audiobooks/files/downloads/`)

<Note>
  Direct file access may require a file manager app. The path is provided for advanced users who want to back up or manage files manually.
</Note>

## Download Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Use WiFi for large downloads**: Audiobooks can be several gigabytes. Connect to WiFi to avoid using mobile data.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Download before traveling**: Prepare your offline library before trips by downloading audiobooks while you have good connectivity.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Manage storage proactively**: Regularly delete finished audiobooks to free up space for new downloads.
</Tip>

## Storage Requirements

Typical audiobook sizes:
- **Short story collections**: 100-300 MB
- **Standard novels**: 300-800 MB
- **Long novels**: 800 MB - 2 GB
- **Epic series or collections**: 2+ GB

Check your device's available storage in your device settings before downloading large audiobooks.
