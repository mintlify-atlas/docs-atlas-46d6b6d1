---
title: Managing Favorites
description: Save and organize your favorite audiobooks for quick access
---

The Favorites feature lets you bookmark audiobooks you love, want to listen to later, or want to recommend to others. Think of it as your personal wishlist.

## Adding to Favorites

Mark any audiobook as a favorite to save it for later:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open audiobook details">
    Browse or search for an audiobook and tap it to open the details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the heart icon">
    In the top-right corner of the screen, tap the **heart icon**. When empty, the audiobook is not in your favorites.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Favorite saved">
    The heart icon fills with red color, indicating the audiobook has been added to your favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can favorite an audiobook even if you haven't started listening to it. Use favorites as a "want to listen" list.
</Tip>

## Viewing Your Favorites

Access your saved audiobooks quickly from the home screen:

1. **Scroll to the Favourites section** on the home screen
2. Your favorite audiobooks appear in a **horizontal scrollable list**
3. Each shows:
   - Cover artwork
   - Title
   - Author name

<Note>
  The Favourites section only appears on the home screen when you have at least one audiobook marked as favorite. If you have no favorites, the section is hidden.
</Note>

## Playing from Favorites

Start listening to a favorite audiobook:

1. Tap any audiobook in the **Favourites section** on the home screen
2. The audiobook detail page opens
3. Tap the **Play button** to start listening

<Tip>
  If you've previously listened to the audiobook, playback resumes from your last position automatically.
</Tip>

## Removing from Favorites

There are two ways to remove audiobooks from your favorites:

### Method 1: From Audiobook Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the audiobook">
    Navigate to the audiobook's detail page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the filled heart">
    Tap the red **heart icon** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Removed from favorites">
    The heart becomes outlined/empty, and the audiobook is removed from your Favourites section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: From Favorites Section

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the audiobook">
    Scroll to the Favourites section on the home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Long press">
    **Press and hold** on the audiobook cover you want to remove.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm removal">
    A dialog appears asking "Are you sure you want to remove this audiobook from favourites?" Tap **Remove**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Removed">
    The audiobook disappears from your Favourites section immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Removing an audiobook from favorites does not delete any downloaded files or affect your listening history. It only removes the bookmark.
</Note>

## Organizing Your Favorites

While Aradia doesn't currently support folders or categories for favorites, here are some tips for keeping your wishlist organized:

<Accordion title="Use favorites for different purposes">
  - **Currently reading**: Keep audiobooks you're actively listening to in favorites for quick access
  - **Want to listen**: Add interesting titles you discover while browsing
  - **Re-listen queue**: Favorite audiobooks you loved and want to hear again
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Regular cleanup">
  Periodically review your favorites and remove audiobooks you've finished or are no longer interested in. This keeps your list focused and useful.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Combine with downloads">
  Favorite audiobooks you plan to download for offline listening. This creates a convenient "download queue" you can work through when on WiFi.
</Accordion>

## Favorites vs. Downloads vs. History

Understand the difference between Aradia's three collection features:

| Feature | Purpose | Location | Limit |
|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| **Favorites** | Manually saved audiobooks you want to keep track of | Home screen Favourites section | Unlimited |
| **Downloads** | Audiobooks saved to device for offline listening | Downloads page (bottom nav) | Limited by device storage |
| **History** | Automatically tracks audiobooks you've played | Home screen Recently Played section | Automatic (most recent) |

<Tip>
  Use all three features together: Favorite audiobooks you love, download them for offline access, and let History automatically track what you've listened to.
</Tip>

## Sharing Favorites

While Aradia doesn't have built-in sharing, you can recommend favorite audiobooks to friends:

1. Open the audiobook from your Favourites
2. Note the **title and author** from the detail page
3. Share this information with friends who also use Aradia or LibriVox
4. They can search for it using the Search feature

<Note>
  All audiobooks in Aradia come from public domain sources (primarily LibriVox), so any title you find will be available to other users as well.
</Note>

## Favorites Persist Across Sessions

Your favorites are stored locally on your device and persist:
- After closing and reopening the app
- After device restarts
- Even when offline

<Note>
  Favorites are stored in the app's local database. If you uninstall Aradia, your favorites will be lost. Consider noting down your favorite titles before uninstalling.
</Note>
