## Overview

This guide will walk you through installing Aradia, browsing the audiobook library, and playing your first audiobook. By the end, you'll be ready to enjoy 20,000+ free audiobooks on your Android device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Aradia">
    First, download and install the Aradia APK on your Android device.
    
    1. Visit the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/sagarchaulagai/aradia/releases)
    2. Download the latest APK (version 3.0.0 or newer)
    3. Enable "Unknown sources" in your device settings
    4. Install the APK
    
    <Card
      title="Need detailed installation help?"
      icon="circle-info"
      href="/installation"
    >
      Check out our comprehensive installation guide for step-by-step instructions and troubleshooting.
    </Card>
    
    <Tip>
      Make sure to grant storage and notification permissions when first launching the app.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Audiobooks">
    Once installed, explore the extensive library of audiobooks.
    
    ### Home Screen Overview
    
    When you open Aradia, you'll see:
    
    - **Welcome Section** - Featured audiobooks and recommendations
    - **Genre Grid** - Quick access to 15+ genres
    - **My Audiobooks** - Your downloaded and favorited titles
    - **History** - Continue listening where you left off
    - **YouTube Import** - Add YouTube videos as audiobooks
    
    ### Browse by Genre
    
    Tap any genre card to explore audiobooks in that category:
    
    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Adventure" icon="compass" />
      <Card title="Biography" icon="user" />
      <Card title="Children" icon="child" />
      <Card title="Comedy" icon="face-laugh" />
      <Card title="Fantasy" icon="wand-sparkle" />
      <Card title="Horror" icon="ghost" />
      <Card title="Mystery" icon="magnifying-glass" />
      <Card title="Philosophy" icon="brain" />
      <Card title="Romance" icon="heart" />
    </CardGroup>
    
    ### Search for Specific Titles
    
    1. Tap the **search icon** in the top navigation bar
    2. Enter a title, author, or subject
    3. Browse the search results
    
    <Note>
      You can search across all 20,000+ audiobooks from the Librivox collection, spanning multiple languages and subjects.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Play Your First Audiobook">
    Let's listen to an audiobook!
    
    ### Select an Audiobook
    
    1. Browse or search for an audiobook that interests you
    2. Tap on the audiobook cover to open the **details page**
    
    ### Audiobook Details
    
    On the details page, you'll see:
    
    - **Cover art** and metadata (title, author, duration)
    - **Description** of the audiobook content
    - **Track list** with all chapters/sections
    - **Play button** to start streaming immediately
    - **Download button** to save for offline listening
    - **Favorite button** (heart icon) to add to your wishlist
    
    ### Start Streaming
    
    Tap the **Play** button to start streaming the audiobook immediately.
    
    <Check>
      The audiobook will begin playing, and you'll see the **mini player** appear at the bottom of the screen.
    </Check>
    
    ### Player Controls
    
    Tap the mini player to open the **full player screen** with advanced controls:
    
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Basic Controls">
        - **Play/Pause** - Control playback
        - **Skip Forward/Back** - Jump 30 seconds (customizable)
        - **Previous/Next Track** - Navigate between chapters
        - **Progress Bar** - Seek to any position
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Advanced Features">
        - **Playback Speed** - Adjust from 0.5x to 3.0x
        - **Sleep Timer** - Auto-pause after a set duration
        - **Skip Silence** - Automatically skip long pauses
        - **Equalizer** - Customize audio settings
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Additional Options">
        - **Track List** - View and jump to specific chapters
        - **Characters** - See narrator and character information
        - **Chromecast** - Cast to TV or smart speakers
        - **Share** - Share the audiobook with friends
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
    
    <Tip>
      Enable **Skip Silence** for a smoother listening experience, especially for audiobooks with long pauses between chapters.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download for Offline Listening">
    Save audiobooks to your device to listen without internet.
    
    ### Download an Audiobook
    
    1. Open the audiobook details page
    2. Tap the **Download** button (download icon)
    3. The app will start downloading all chapters
    4. Track download progress in the **Downloads** tab
    
    ### Manage Downloads
    
    Access your downloads:
    
    1. Tap the **hamburger menu** (three lines) in the top-left
    2. Select **Downloads** from the navigation menu
    3. View all downloaded audiobooks
    4. Tap any audiobook to play offline
    
    <Warning>
      Large audiobooks can take significant storage space (500 MB - 2 GB). Make sure you have enough free space before downloading.
    </Warning>
    
    ### Delete Downloads
    
    To free up space:
    
    1. Long-press on a downloaded audiobook
    2. Tap **Delete** to remove it from your device
    3. You can re-download it anytime from the library
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Favorites">
    Keep track of audiobooks you love or want to listen to later.
    
    ### Add to Wishlist
    
    1. Open the audiobook details page
    2. Tap the **heart icon** to add to favorites
    3. The heart will turn filled/colored to confirm
    
    ### View Favorites
    
    1. Go to the **Home** screen
    2. Scroll to the **Favorites Section**
    3. All your favorited audiobooks appear here
    
    <Tip>
      Use favorites to create a reading list or bookmark audiobooks you discover while browsing.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bonus Features

Now that you know the basics, explore these additional features:

### Import YouTube Videos as Audiobooks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open YouTube Import">
    Tap the **YouTube Import** section on the home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter YouTube URL">
    Paste a YouTube video URL into the input field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Import and Play">
    Tap **Import** and the video will be added to your library as an audiobook. Perfect for lectures, podcasts, or any audio content on YouTube.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  YouTube import extracts only the audio from videos, saving bandwidth and battery life.
</Note>

### Import Local Audiobook Files

Have audiobook files on your device? Import them into Aradia:

1. Tap the **Local Imports** section on the home screen
2. Select **Import from Files**
3. Choose audiobook files (MP3, M4A, etc.) from your device
4. Aradia will automatically organize them with metadata

### Browse Audiobooks by Language

Aradia supports 20+ languages:

1. Open the app **Settings**
2. Select **Language Filter**
3. Choose your preferred language
4. Browse audiobooks in English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Russian, and more

### Continue Listening Across Devices

Your listening progress is automatically saved:

- **History Section** on the home screen shows recent audiobooks
- Tap any audiobook to resume exactly where you left off
- Progress is saved locally on your device

## Tips for the Best Experience

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Optimize Battery Life" icon="battery-full">
    - Download audiobooks for offline listening instead of streaming
    - Lower the screen brightness while listening
    - Use the sleep timer to avoid all-night playback
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Improve Audio Quality" icon="volume-high">
    - Open the full player and tap the **equalizer icon**
    - Adjust bass, treble, and other settings to your preference
    - Use headphones or external speakers for better sound
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customize Playback" icon="sliders">
    - Adjust playback speed (1.25x - 1.5x is popular for faster listening)
    - Change skip forward/back duration in settings (default: 30 seconds)
    - Enable skip silence for audiobooks with long pauses
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Discover New Content" icon="compass">
    - Browse the **Recommendations** section for curated picks
    - Explore different genres you haven't tried
    - Use the search to find audiobooks by your favorite authors
    - Check the **New Arrivals** section for recently added titles
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Background Playback" icon="mobile">
    - Audio continues playing when you switch apps
    - Control playback from the notification shade
    - Use the lock screen controls without unlocking your device
    - The mini player persists across all app screens
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
