## Your Gateway to Free Audiobooks

Aradia is a free, ad-free mobile application offering seamless access to over **20,000 audiobooks** from Librivox using the Archive.org API. Built with passion using Flutter and Dart, Aradia is designed for literature enthusiasts who want to enjoy classic literature in audio format without interruptions or costs.

<Note>
  Aradia is completely free and open-source. No subscriptions, no ads, no tracking—just pure listening pleasure.
</Note>

## Supported Languages

Aradia supports audiobooks in over 20 languages:

- **English** - 20,000+ titles
- **German** (Deutsch) - 1,500+ titles
- **Spanish** (Español) - 800+ titles  
- **French** (Français) - 1,200+ titles
- **Portuguese** (Português) - 400+ titles
- **Italian** (Italiano) - 600+ titles
- **Russian** (Русский) - 500+ titles
- **Dutch** (Nederlands) - 300+ titles
- **Polish** (Polski) - 200+ titles
- And many more including Chinese, Japanese, Greek, Hebrew, Latin, Finnish, Swedish, Catalan, Danish, and Esperanto

<Tip>
  Use the language filter in the app to browse audiobooks in your preferred language.
</Tip>
